AAUA HISTORY

The story of Adekunle Ajasin University, Akungba-Akoko (AAUA), Ondo State, Nigeria, is more appropriately told in two phases. It was first established as Obafemi Awolowo University in March 1982n by the government of the old Ondo State, headed by the late Chief Adekunle Ajasin.
